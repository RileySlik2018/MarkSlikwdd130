Breathtaking sites

“ I came on this trip hoping to make wonderful memories with my family. Our daughter was too young to go on any of the more extreme rafting trips but luckily our tour guide took us on the perfect little adventure. He took us to this chasm that was covered wall to wall in little waterfalls and plants. At the end of the chasm was a pool of water with a small beach that I stopped at for lunch, it was honestly one the best trips I have ever taken with my family. Between the amazing sites and great staff I would definitely come again”- not an AI person , Dave
